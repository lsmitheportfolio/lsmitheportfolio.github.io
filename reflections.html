Real-World Issue Annotated Bibliography

January 2019

    As a world economic leader, the US has a booming manufacturing sector. Between 1870 and 2014, manufacturing increased twentyfold, going up an average of 8% each year (Egilmez et al. 860). Nearly a third of all greenhouse gas emissions are caused by manufacturing, so as the amount of manufacturing increases, so do emissions (Egilmez et al. 854). These are a problem since they contribute to global warming and severely impact the ecosystem. As Earth’s population increases and demand for goods goes up, manufacturing is not likely to decrease. Therefore, it is necessary for manufacturers to examine their carbon footprint and take steps to reduce it. Currently, scientists are developing ways of doing this by optimizing manufacturing processes (Edgar and Davis). In addition, more and more businesses are examining environmental impact when developing new products (Miranda et al. 13065). Despite these improvements, manufacturing sectors with some of the highest emissions, such as petroleum refinement, livestock processing, and alloy production, need to take further steps (Egilmez et al. 853). To reduce emissions in these sectors, scientists are developing new technologies and methods for manufacturing. Engineers are tackling the problem of greenhouse gas emissions through both general and targeted solutions.

College Options

August 2018

I’m proficient in information literacy and I hope to develop written communication.

Information literacy is useful in a number of situations. When writing research papers, knowing how to find good resources is important. This skill helps me find enough information even for long papers and it ensures that I present accurate information. This eases the difficulty of writing research papers and improves their quality. Information literacy also helps me with programming. Troubleshooting a program can be tedious, and finding the solution online is not straightforward. I use information literacy to narrow down my problem, find the source if it, and then find the solution. Also, if I get lost in a class, this skill can allow me find online resources that can help. When I do undergraduate research or internships, this skill can help me learn more on my topic of study. Information literacy will help me in my college career.

My written communication skills can be improved with practice. Taking a writing class is an important step in improving. I should also try to improve from all writing assignments, not just ones from an English class. Visiting the writing center will help with this. By taking each writing assignment as a learning experience, I can develop this skill.

In my future career, written communication and information literacy will be important. Employers want employees to problem solve, and the internet is an important resource for this. Information literacy will help me navigate the internet to find my answers. Written communication is important in many aspects. It’s necessary for getting hired, working a group, and explaining work to others. Both skills are important in the workplace.
